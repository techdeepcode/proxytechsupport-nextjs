IT Job Support in Austria & German-Speaking Europe

Austrian IT Market Context

Austria's tech sector is concentrated in Vienna and growing in Graz and Linz. Key industries:

  • Banking and insurance (Vienna is a major European financial hub)
  • Manufacturing automation and Industry 4.0
  • Tourism and hospitality technology
  • Government IT and digital identity systems

Technologies in Demand in Austria

  • Java and Spring – enterprise and banking backends
  • .NET and C# – large in Austrian government and insurance projects
  • SAP – extensive usage across Austrian manufacturing
  • Azure – Microsoft is deeply embedded in Austrian enterprise
  • DevOps (Kubernetes, Terraform, Azure DevOps) – digital transformation initiatives

European Working Hours

Austria observes CET (UTC+1) / CEST (UTC+2). Our afternoon IST team overlaps perfectly with Austrian morning and midday working hours.
