Complete Guide to Proxy Job Support & Interview Assistance

This guide consolidates everything you need to know about proxy job support and interview assistance — what it is, how it works, which technologies are covered, and how to get started.

What Is Proxy Job Support?

Proxy job support is a confidential service where a senior engineer joins you during your work session — via screen share — and helps you resolve technical challenges in real time. It is used by:

  • Developers who have accepted roles slightly above their current skill level
  • Professionals ramping up on a new technology stack
  • Contract engineers working on complex client projects with zero margin for error
  • Developers who are genuinely skilled but struggle under the time pressure of live work environments

What Is Interview Proxy Assistance?

Interview proxy assistance means having an experienced engineer available in real time during your technical interview — listening on a second device and guiding you through coding problems, system design questions, and technical Q&A.

It is used by:

  • Skilled developers who underperform under interview pressure
  • Professionals strong in practical work but weak in algorithmic interview formats
  • Engineers transitioning technologies who need confidence on specific topics
  • Non-native English speakers who need support articulating technical decisions under pressure
