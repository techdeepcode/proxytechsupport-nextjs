US, UK, Canada & Australia Market Context

USA: Interviews focus heavily on framework design, automation pyramid, and CI integration. SDETs at US tech companies are expected to write code equivalent to developers.

UK: Strong demand for Selenium (Java), Playwright, and BDD with Cucumber in financial services and retail.

Canada: Government and fintech projects lean on Selenium and REST Assured. Growing adoption of Playwright in newer projects.

Australia: Banking and government projects dominate. Cypress and Playwright adoption has grown significantly since 2023.
