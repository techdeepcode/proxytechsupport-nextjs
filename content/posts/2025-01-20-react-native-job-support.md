## React Native Job Support – Mobile App Development Help

Building a cross-platform mobile app sounds simple until you hit a platform-specific crash, a Flipper debugger that won't connect, or a native module that compiles fine on iOS but breaks on Android. Our **React Native job support** service gives you live help from engineers who ship React Native apps to production every week.

**Expo Ecosystem**

**Performance & Debugging**
- JS bundle size reduction – Metro bundler configuration

- Flipper plugin setup and debugging

**Testing & Deployment**
- Jest + React Native Testing Library

- App Store Connect and Google Play deployment
- OTA updates with Expo Updates or CodePush
